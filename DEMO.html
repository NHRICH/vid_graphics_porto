🌓 Dark Mode Demo

Welcome to the dark mode demonstration page! This page showcases the new dark mode feature added to the Nahom Portfolio.

👉 Look at the bottom-right corner and click the toggle button! ↘️

What You'll See

When you click the toggle button:

  • All colors smoothly transition to dark mode
  • The toggle icon changes from 🌙 (moon) to ☀️ (sun)
  • Your preference is automatically saved
  • The theme persists across all pages
  • Everything remains perfectly readable

Key Features

  • Persistent - Your choice is remembered
  • Smooth - Beautiful transitions
  • Accessible - Keyboard navigation supported
  • Responsive - Works on all devices
  • Fast - Instant toggle response

Try It Now!

Click the circular button in the bottom-right corner to toggle between light and dark modes. Watch how smoothly everything transitions!

Keyboard Users

You can also use keyboard navigation:

  1. Press Tab to navigate to the toggle button
  2. Press Enter or Space to toggle
